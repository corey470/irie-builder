# Irie Experience Builder

> "Like a good book you can't put down — where every scroll reveals something new, and the story just keeps getting better."

The first tool that starts with what you want someone to feel — and builds everything else around that.

## What It Is

Not a website builder. An experience builder.

Every other tool asks: "What kind of page do you want to build?"

Irie Experience Builder asks: "What do you want someone to feel?"

Then it builds everything — the motion, the pacing, the story arc, the visual atmosphere — around that feeling. Using everything it already knows about the brand.

## The Vision

Websites that are alive. Not animated in the sense of things moving around a screen. Alive in the sense of a good book you can't put down. Where every scroll reveals something new. Where the story gets better the further you go. Where you don't read the brand — you feel it.

## Three Modes

- **Mode A — Visionary:** Describe the feeling. It builds everything. No decisions required.
- **Mode B — Collaborator:** You guide. It executes. You refine.
- **Mode C — Architect:** You direct. It codes. You ship.
