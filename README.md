# Irie Experience Builder

> "Like a good book you can't put down — where every scroll reveals something new, and the story just keeps getting better."

The first tool that starts with what you want someone to feel — and builds everything else around that.

## What It Is

Not a website builder. An experience builder.

Every other tool asks: **"What kind of page do you want to build?"**

Irie Experience Builder asks: **"What do you want someone to feel?"**

Then it builds everything — the motion, the pacing, the story arc, the visual atmosphere — around that feeling. Using everything it already knows about the brand.

## The Problem

Everyone can build a website. A five-year-old can build a website today. The tools are everywhere, free, and they all produce the same thing: pages that display information and wait to be read.

The brands that break through aren't built on templates. They're built on feeling. They make you want to stay.

## The Vision

Websites that are alive. Not animated in the sense of things moving around a screen. Alive in the sense of a good book you can't put down. Where every scroll reveals something new. Where the story gets better the further you go. Where you don't read the brand — you feel it.

## Three Modes

**Mode A — Visionary**
Describe the feeling. It builds everything. No decisions required.
For operators who have the vision but not the time.

**Mode B — Collaborator**
You guide. It executes. You refine.
For creatives who want control over the story.

**Mode C — Architect**
You direct. It codes. You ship.
For developers and designers who want AI handling execution.

## What It Is Not

- Not a template picker
- Not a drag-and-drop editor
- Not copy you paste somewhere else
- Not a theme customizer
- Not a clone of someone else's site
- Not something you replicate with a single prompt

## What It Is

- An AI that reads your brand voice, products, and story before you type a word
- A tool that builds structure, motion, pacing, and copy around an emotional brief
- A generator of real, deployed, living web experiences
- A creative collaborator that makes decisions like a great art director
- Something that could only exist for that specific brand
- Future-proof — built on craft and emotion, not trends that age out

## The Input

The intelligence comes from what it already has access to through Irie OS:

- **Already knows:** Brand voice, tone, language, values
- **Already knows:** Products, visuals, colors from Mockup Studio
- **Already knows:** Collections, pricing, story from store data
- **You provide:** "What do you want someone to feel?"

One question. Everything else flows from the answer.

## The Output

- Scroll-triggered animations and motion
- Story architecture — the AI decides the order the story unfolds
- Visual atmosphere calibrated to the emotional brief
- Brand copy written in your voice
- Live deployment at your URL — not downloaded, not exported

## What Makes It Different

| Every other tool | Irie Experience Builder |
|---|---|
| Starts with structure | Starts with emotion |
| Generic by default | Brand-specific by design |
| Copy to paste somewhere | Live experience, deployed |
| Static output | Connected intelligence |
| Ages with trends | Built on craft that endures |

## Build Order

1. Deploy Smart Website Builder module from Irie Modules into Ziggy Dashboard
2. Connect to Brand Voice and store data
3. Add motion and animation output — living pages, not copy
4. Implement three modes — users find their level naturally
5. Live deployment pipeline — published at brand URL on click

## The North Star

> "Like a good book you can't put down — where every scroll reveals something new, and the story just keeps getting better."

Every build decision gets measured against this. Does it make the experience more alive? Does it make you want to keep scrolling? If not, it doesn't ship.
